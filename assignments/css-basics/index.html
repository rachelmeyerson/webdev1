Summary

The Devil Wears Prada follows determined New Yorker, Andy Sachs, through her journey working at Runway fashion magazine. Athough she hopes to one day become a journalist, she perservers through the brutal world of the fashion industry to get there. On call 24/7, she reports to the Editor in Cheif of Runway, Miranda Priestly, alongside, Emily Charlton and Nigel. As she learns to excel at work, her personal relationships with her friends, family, and boyfriend, Nate, start to crumble.

Cast

This cast is full of icons that help make this movie what it is. Meryl Streep, Anne Hathaway, Emily Blunt, and Stanley Tucci are truly the stars of the film. The way they interact with each other makes you think they have known each other for years. Other supporting stars include Tracie Thoms, Adrian Grenier, and Simon Baker,

Why I Love It

The Devil Wears Prada is my favorite movie of all time. Not only is it just an overall fantastic feel good movie, it was also a huge part of my childhood. My mon, my sister, and I would watch this movie over and over again, to the point that I can now recite the majority of the words. It has an incredible cast, that makes fans fall in love with their characters, which keeps us coming back again and again.

The Devil Wears Prada, the greatest movie of all time.
